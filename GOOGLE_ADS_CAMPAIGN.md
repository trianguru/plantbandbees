# Google Ads Campaign Guide - PlantBandBees

Complete setup guide for launching Google Search Ads to acquire waiting list signups for PlantBandBees in Knoxville, TN.

**Budget:** $300-600/month ($10-20/day)
**Target Cost Per Lead:** $8-15
**Geographic Target:** Knoxville, TN + 30-mile radius
**Campaign Objective:** Waiting list signups (conversions)

---

## Step 1: Google Ads Account Setup

### 1.1 Create Account

1. Go to [ads.google.com](https://ads.google.com)
2. Click **Start Now**
3. Choose **Switch to Expert Mode** (important!)
4. Account type: **Business**
5. Billing country: **United States**
6. Time zone: **(UTC-05:00) Eastern Time**
7. Currency: **USD ($)**

### 1.2 Set Up Billing

1. Go to **Tools & Settings** → **Billing**
2. Add payment method (credit card recommended)
3. Set billing threshold: $50 (or automatic monthly)
4. Confirm billing info

### 1.3 Link Google Analytics (Optional but Recommended)

1. Go to **Tools & Settings** → **Linked accounts**
2. Click **Google Analytics (UA/GA4)**
3. Link your GA4 property
4. Enable auto-tagging

## Step 11: Ongoing Optimization (Weekly)

### Week 1-2: Data Collection

**DON'T make changes yet.** Let the algorithm learn.

Monitor:
- Search terms report (what triggered your ads?)
- Quality Score (aim for 7+)
- Impression share
- Click-through rate

**Add negative keywords** if seeing irrelevant searches.

### Week 3-4: Initial Optimization

**Pause underperforming:**
- Ads with CTR < 1% (after 1000 impressions)
- Keywords with CTR < 0.5% (after 500 impressions)
- No conversions after $50 spend

**Increase bids on:**
- Keywords with conversions
- Ad groups with low impression share

**Create new ad variations** for top performers.

### Month 2+: Scale What Works

**Budget increases:**
- If CPL < $12, increase budget 20%
- If conversion rate > 15%, increase budget
- Never increase more than 20% at once

**Expand keywords:**
- Add close variants of winners
- Test broader match types cautiously

**Switch bidding strategy:**
- After 50+ conversions, switch to **Target CPA**
- Set target: $10-12 (based on your actual CPL)

## Troubleshooting

### Low Impressions

**Causes:**
- Budget too low
- Bids too low
- Keywords too specific
- Low search volume

**Solutions:**
- Increase daily budget to $20
- Add broader match keywords
- Expand geographic radius to 40 miles
- Add more keyword variations

### High Cost Per Click

**Causes:**
- Competitive keywords
- Low quality score
- Broad match overuse

**Solutions:**
- Focus on phrase/exact match
- Improve landing page relevance
- Add negative keywords
- Test less competitive keywords

### Low Conversion Rate

**Causes:**
- Wrong audience
- Landing page issues
- Offer not compelling

**Solutions:**
- Check search terms (adding negatives)
- A/B test landing page
- Update ad copy to set expectations
- Increase early bird discount (25%?)

### Budget Depletes Too Quickly

**Causes:**
- Bids too high
- Broad match burning budget
- Low quality score

**Solutions:**
- Lower bids manually
- Switch to manual CPC bidding
- Use more restrictive match types
- Improve quality score

## Advanced Strategies (Month 3+)

### Remarketing Lists for Search Ads (RLSA)

1. Create audience: "Website visitors (past 30 days)"
2. Add to campaign as "observation"
3. Increase bids 20-50% for returning visitors

### Audience Targeting

Layer these audiences (observation mode):

- Small business owners
- Property managers
- Real estate investors
- Home & garden enthusiasts

### Ad Scheduling

After collecting data:
- Identify best-performing hours/days
- Increase bids during peak times
- Decrease or pause during off-hours

### Dynamic Search Ads

Let Google automatically create ads based on your website content:
- Good for catching long-tail searches
- Lower maintenance
- Can discover new keywords
